Why use a standing desk?
The truth behind standing desks

Are you reading this while standing at your desk? There's a good chance that you are — standing desks are all the rage. Standing desks, which are also referred to as height-adjustable desks, sit-stand desks, or stand-up desks, is essentially a desk that lets you draw, write, and work while standing up or using a high stool. There are several diffeproduct types, which allows you to get the one that fits your office and budget the best.
